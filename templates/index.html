Welcome to Pixel Domination

Created by Mark Arguinbaev, Cal Blanco, Will Hord, Isaac Leiva, and Frank Podraza.

Choose or Create Your Own Server

Specify the size, time between placements, and how long the game is live, or simply join an existing server.

Pick a Color

Your color is your team. Choose either black, red, green, blue, or yellow to represent and fight for control over the board. Once your choice is made, and your first pixel is placed, your color will be locked for the duration of the game board.

Dominate the Board

Amass as much territory as possible by claiming new pixels adjacent to any existing pixels of your color. Either expand into a blank space, or go on the offensive and steal another team's pixel. Whichever color has the most points scored when the clock hits zero is the victor.
